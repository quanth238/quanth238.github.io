Current research directions include birth-death structural learning for 3D Gaussian Splatting, splat regression models for particle density control in radiance fields, optimization for learned 3D representations, and render-aware subset selection for post-training compaction.

The common theme is efficient 3D scene representation: methods that keep visual quality high while improving controllability, geometry, or compactness.
